Hello

I'm a senior software engineer at Hireology. I primarily work in Ruby, but have experience programming in Clojure, R, Java, Javascript, Racket (scheme) and Common Lisp.

Prior to Hireology, I was a software craftsman at 8th Light. I had the great privilege to apprentice under Micah Martin. The apprenticeship experience can be summarized as learning in depth the lessons and principles in Uncle Bob's Patterns, Principles and Practices book, including the SOLID principles and package / component principles. Those ideas were transformative in my thinking about how to write software and I rely on them everyday to guide my thought process. I'm probably what DHH would consider a zealot about TDD. I'm fine with that.

In past lives, I graduated with degrees in Religious Studies and Japanese Language. I lived in East Asia for three years as a student, an English teacher and a teacher trainer (in that order). I have also spent a considerable amount of time studying environmental policy. I lived like a Buddhist monk for a couple years during my early 20's, and was fortunate to enjoy several meditation retreats. Although now I would not claim to be a card carrying Buddhist, I value meditation, compassion, loving-kindness and the daily mindful effort to enjoy and help others enjoy the simple feeling of being.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy attempting to make the best homemade salsa in the world. I also enjoy making candles. I can play piano, bass and guitar, and enjoy putzing around with a homemade monome.
